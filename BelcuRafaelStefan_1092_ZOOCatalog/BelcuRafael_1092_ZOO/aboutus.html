TRANSYLVANIA WILDLIFE LOOKOUT ZOO

Located at 13 km from Râșnov town at Southwest of Brasov city, Romania, Transylvania Wildlife Lookout ZOO is a Zoological Garden with a total area of 49.5 ha.

The ZOO represents a public facility that maintains and exposes live, wild, indigenous collections of animals and wishes to achieve two main goals: preservation of fauna (with priority to endangered species, some which are rescued) that is reprensetative to this area; and training, education and recreation of the visitor audience.

The Wildlife includes the species such as: Brown Bear, Chamois, Eurasian lynx, European green lizard ,Golden jackal, European wildcat, Carpathian boar, Fallow deer, Red deer, Red fox, European Bison, Grey wolf.
